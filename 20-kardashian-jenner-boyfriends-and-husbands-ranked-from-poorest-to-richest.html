20 Kardashian-Jenner Boyfriends And Husbands Ranked, From Poorest To Richest

Highlights

  • The Kardashian-Jenner women have a history of dating athletes and bad boy rappers, but recent relationships show a change in their preferences.
  • Many of the men they have dated or married are young and wealthy, with net worths in the millions of dollars.
  • While some relationships were short-lived, others lasted longer and resulted in children. The women are now focusing on raising their children and not closed off to finding love again.

The Kardashian-Jenner women have dated (a lot) and gotten married and divorced (quite a bit). Kim Kardashian, Kourtney Kardashian, and Khloe Kardashian, together with Kylie Jenner and Kendall Jenner seemed to have a "thing" for athletes, basketball players being the preferred species, and usually bad boy rappers once upon a time, a fact that appears to have changed in recent days. They hook up with guys, occasionally producing babies who are given celebrity-appropriate names like Stormi or True. Sometimes the guys are nice. Sometimes they are schemers and cheaters. For the most part, they come and go pretty quickly.

Most of the guys on this list are young and up-and-coming and run in the same circles as the infamous sisters. Some of them haven't had much money. But most of them are, like the Kardashian-Jenners, rolling in millions of dollars of net worth. So, let's take a look at 20 guys the sisters have dated and/or married and (sometimes) divorced. There's a lot of money on the list.

Updated August 2023: The Kardashian and Jenner sisters have had their fair share of high-profile relationships over the years. Some of them seem like good matches, while others appear to be polar opposites.

While Kourtney has been in the headlines most recently because of her marriage to Travis Barker and a subsequent baby on the way, Kendall and her relationship with Bad Bunny have tongues wagging as well.

Perhaps having learned about having a relationship play out in public, Kylie is keeping her relationship with Timothee Chalamet quiet.

For Kim, it appears that while rumors about her dating Tom Brady are unfounded, she, as well as Khloe are not closed off to finding love again. But currently, look to be putting their attention to raising their children.

20 Harry Hudson - $5 Million

Kourtney dated Harry Hudson when she was 40 and he was 26. Hudson's a folk/rock singer whose first album was released by Jay-Z's Roc Nation label.

The relationship would ultimately not last. And with a $5 million net worth, Harry has the least amount of the Kardashian/Jenner exes on the list.

19 Pete Davidson: $8 Million

The relationship between Pete Davidson and Kim caught a lot of people off guard. But, the two looked like they were not only enjoying one another's time when together, given the beef that Pete and Kanye West were getting into via Twitter, the relationship looked to be taking a turn for the serious.

Unfortunately, Pete and Kim split 10 months after being linked, reportedly because Kim's intense diet became too difficult for Pete to deal with. But at the end of the day, with an $8 million net worth, Pete is doing just fine.

18 Ray- J: $14 Million

Back when rapper Ray J and young Kim were "dating" they had occasion to make that 2003 video. You know. The one that catapulted Kim (and the Kardashians) into fame and fortune status. Rapper Ray J is singer Brandy's brother. Kim moved on from Ray J, but not before the video did its magic. Ray J is worth $14 million.

17 Nick Lachey - $25 Million

Blink and you would have missed it. Nick and Kim hooked up briefly in 2006. This was pre-Keeping Up With the Kardashians days. Nick is one of a number of celebrities who have accused Kim of associating with celebrities in order to shine in the spotlight and has had other unkind things to say about the reality star as well.

Like Kim, Nick continues to grow his net worth. Most recently, his fortune grew as the host of Love is Blind with his wife, Vanessa Lachey. Nick is currently worth $25 million.

16 Timothee Chalamet: $25 Million

While the two have been very low-key with their relationship, Timothee Chalamet and Kylie have been linked for a handful of months. Though things are "casual" between the two right now, the two are seeing where things go with their relationship by not allowing the spotlight to cause drama between them.

This is a smart move on the couple's part, given that Timothee is becoming increasingly popular by the day with his acting career taking off, which has earned him a net worth of $25 million.

15 Reggie Bush - $25 Million

Kim and the NFL player Reggie Bush dated from 2007 to 2010. The two lived their lives in front of the camera when Reggie was in his off-season or when Kim came to visit him for his games. This lasted until 2010 when the two broke up.

Reggie went on to marry Lilit Avagyan in 2014 and has been happily married since. And he definitely isn't hurting for cash with a net worth of $25 million.

14 Joe Francis - $25 Million

Producer Joe Francis and Kourtney hooked up for a bit in 2005. Rumor has it that Joe, no stranger to "adult" content, helped Kim get that Ray J tape into the right hands to maximize publicity.

Joe's brand Girls Gone Wild went bankrupt and he was eventually convicted of tax evasion. By that time, Kourtney had moved on from Joe.

Since that time, Joe has lived in Mexico in his palatial estate, where he still has a net worth of $25 million.

13 Lamar Odom - $30 Million

The September 2009 wedding of Khloe and NBA player Lamar Odom was broadcast as a special event on Keeping Up With the Kardashians. But by 2013, citing Lamar's infidelity, Khloe had called time and filed for divorce.

The divorce was delayed after Lamar suffered an overdose but was finally finalized in 2016. Since then, Lamar has invested in Odom Wellness Treatment Centers to help others suffering from addiction. Lamar is worth $30 million.

12 Kris Humphries - $35 Million

Talk about publicity! Kim got engaged to NBA star Kris Humphries in May of 2011, and they married in August of that year. The wedding was televised and fans nationwide watched. Critics, of course, accused Kim of using the association and wedding to up her profile.

The marriage between Kim and Kris lasted 72 days. Enough said.

Kris has since retired from the NBA and is worth $35 million.

11 Bad Bunny: $40 Million

Linked since February 2023, Kendall and Bad Bunny, aka, Benito Antonio Martinez Ocasio, have been looking more serious about one another in more recent months.

There is no telling if Bad Bunny is the one that will cause Kendall to settle down, but with a net worth of $40 million that continues to grow, the two can have many adventures together while the figure out the direction they want their relationship to go.

10 Tristan Thompson - $45 Million

Khloe hooked up with NBA basketball star Tristan Thompson in 2016. While they were dating, Khloe got pregnant. Days before she was due, he cheated on her. However, Khloe decided to give him a second chance. That turned out to be a mistake after Tristan cheated on her with Jordyn Woods.

Khloe and Tristan found their way back to each other in the years after and decided to have a baby via a surrogate. But, Tristan went back to his old ways and cheated again, ending the relationship permanently. Tristan is worth $45 million.

9 Scott Disick - $45 Million

Kourtney and actor/producer Scott Disick were together from 2006 to 2015 and had three kids together. They never married. Their turns on Keeping Up With the Kardashians looked like happy families personified. But by 2015, Scott's fondness for a drink or two and his partying ways spelled the end of the relationship.

Scott took his $45 million and hooked up with model Sofia Richie shortly thereafter, but it did not last. And unfortunately, the ladies that Scott was connected to in the years after did not last either. Here's to hoping Scott will eventually find the one.

8 Devin Booker: $50 Million

Kendall and Devin Booker were linked romantically for two years. The two learned how to navigate a relationship in the public eye together and looked like they might just go the distance. Unfortunately, with their busy schedules, this was just not meant to be.

Devin seems to be putting all of his efforts these days into his basketball career, where he has earned a net worth of $50 million.

7 Nick Cannon - $50 Million

Kim and actor/singer Nick Cannon dated in 2006-2007. In 2012, he told Howard Stern that they broke up because Kim denied making her tape with Ray J. Then the tape with Ray J came out and the relationship ended.

Since then, Nick has gone on to marry and divorce Mariah Carey and has 12 children with several different women. Nick is worth $50 million.

6 Travis Barker: $50 Million

Travis Barker and Kourtney was a relationship that initially did not make sense to fans. The two seemed like polar opposites.

With reports about the fact that Travis really wanted to date Kim and that he was controlling over Kourtney, no one thought the relationship would last. However, the two proved everyone wrong by getting married in 2022 and now have a baby on the way.

Travis continues to grow his net worth of $50 million by touring again with his band, Blink 182.

5 Travis Scott - $80 Million

Kylie met singer Travis Scott in 2017. Sparks flew and pretty soon she was seen on tour with him. Their daughter Stormi was born in 2018. Then came the cheating rumors. The couple split in 2019, officially denying Travis's cheating ways had anything to do with it.

However, the two reconciled and had a second baby, Arie, in 2022. But that was short-lived as the two have gone their separate ways in the months after. Travis is worth $80 million.

4 Blake Griffin - $110 Million

What is it with the Kardashian-Jenners and football and basketball player? Boston Celtics' player Blake Griffin hung out with Kendall Jenner in 2017 and 2018. They were seen at concerts and parties together and rumors swirled that they were getting serious about one another. This, however, proved to not be the case.

Blake has done a bit of film acting, which only added to his $110 million net worth.

3 Harry Styles - $120 Million

Take one high-profile supermodel and pair her with a rock musician and what have you got? A heck of a lot of media and social media attention.

Kendall and One Direction's Harry Styles dated in 2013 before splitting in 2014. There were rumors along the way of the two reconciling, none of which came to fruition.

Since that time, Harry has continued to build his net worth and is doing quite well for himself with a cool $120 million fortune.

2 James Harden - $165 Million

Khloe and James Harden met at Kanye West's birthday party in 2015. While there was chemistry between the two, the Houston Rockets basketball player lived in Houston. And as a result of the long-distance relationship proving to be too tough for James to remain exclusive, he cheated on Khloe. Shortly thereafter, the two broke up.

James reportedly has a net worth of $165 million.

1 Kanye West - $500 Million

It looked like marriage number three was the lucky one for Kim. She and Kanye West married in Italy in 2014 and went on to have four children together.

Unfortunately, the two separated and officially divorced in 2022. Since then, Kanye got married to Bianca Censori.

While Kanye's controversial comments have gotten him in trouble with companies to the point where he no longer collaborates with several of them, he is still worth a pretty penny with a net worth of $500 million.
