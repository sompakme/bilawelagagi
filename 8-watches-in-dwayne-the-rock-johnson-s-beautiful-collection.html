8 Watches In Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson's Beautiful Collection

Dwayne Johnson is a former wrestler who found his calling as an actor and producer. He is also a highly successful philanthropist and businessman who loves to treat his fans to action-packed blockbuster movies. He is one of the top actors featured on the Forbes list of highest-paid actors for 2019 and 2020. From the Fast And The Furious franchise to Jumanji and Jungle Cruise, 'The Rock' dominates the box office. The actor heavily promotes his projects during press junkets and late-night talk shows when people see his style. Ilaria Urbinati has been the Rock’s stylist for a while, and she is one of the people who has influenced his fashion over the years. One of the most important elements of his fashion is his timepieces.

With his $400 million net worth, he buys luxurious properties and accessories that go with his style. His watch collection suits his personality as he uses chunky timepieces with bold designs. Let’s take a look at Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson’s watch collection.

MTM Special Ops. Cobra - $1,850

Dwayne Johnson usually wears stylish watches for his action-packed movies that can survive the dangerous stunts performed by him. According to Watch ID, the actor was wearing an MTM Special Ops. Cobra watched during his shoots of the 2018 movies Rampage and Skyscraper. The Cobra timepiece worn by him costs $1,850 and comes with an option between 44mm or 47.5mm dials. The black dial, as sported by Johnson, comes with a matching Ballistic black strap.

Oris Pro Diver - $4,650

While Johnson is known to wear Italian brand Panerai for his movies, the Swiss brand Oris is also a good choice. The Rock wore the Pro Diver model in 2016 while shooting Fast And The Furious 6. Oris developed the chunky watch after taking the opinions of professional divers who suggested an RSS Safety System that ensures the bezel is locked underwater. His watch costs $4,650 and has a lightweight titanium case and second-time zone.

IWC Schaffhausen Big Pilot Heritage - $7,363

Big watch for a big man, the Big Pilot Heritage by IWC comes with a 46mm titanium case with a brown calfskin strap. The water-resistant timepiece has sapphire glass with an anti-reflective coating. The 168-hour power reserve watch has a self-winding caliber. According to the British GQ, the watch costs upwards of $7,363. The Rock was spotted wearing the watch in Las Vegas in 2018 when he promoted his movie, Hobbs & Show, at the ComicCon.

Panerai Luminor Submersible 1950 3 Days Automatic Titanio - $9,200

Dwayne Johnson has been spotted wearing the 1950 3 Days Automatic Titanio on multiple occasions. He has worn watches from the Submersible collection since his days shooting Fast Five in 2011, followed by San Andreas in 2013 and most recently, during his press junket for Jumanji: The Next Level in 2019. The titanium watch is equipped to survive the actor's action-packed stunts and has a 72-hour power reserve.

Breitling Transocean Chronograph Unitime - $10,715

One of the most complicated timepieces in Dwayne Johnson’s collection is the Breitling Transocean Chronograph Unitime. Costing $10,715, the outer rim of the watch has the names of 24 major cities listed to represent their time zones, as stated by Watch Ranker. It has daylight saving indicators, and The Rock can easily see any time zones compared to the local time. He was spotted wearing this watch during his HBO TV Show Ballers, running for five seasons until 2019.

Rolex Sea-Dweller - $14,590

The actor was spotted wearing the Rolex Sea-Dweller during his post-production and press for the 2017 action-comedy movie Baywatch. The 43mm stainless steel timepiece costs $14,590 and comes with a sapphire crystal and a unidirectional rotating bezel. It is equipped with an automatic caliber and has a power reserve of 70 hours. Just like Panerai watches, the Sea-Dweller has a black dial and shiny hour hands and markers.

IWC Portugieser Annual Calendar - $20,900

When Dwayne Johnson had to attend the premiere of Netflix’s highest budget film Red Notice in November 2021, The Rock decided to sport a classy watch on his wrist. Made by IWC, the $20,900 watch comes with a 44mm diameter polished case. The stainless steel watch has a blue dial, making it a comfortable piece for casual or formal events. As the name suggests, the watch has an Annual Calendar on the top center and two discs for days and seconds.

Rolex GMT Master II ‘Root Beer’ - $21,936

One of his recent watches is the most underrated timepiece from Rolex’s GMT Master II collection. According to D Marge, the watch comes from a coveted collection when the pieces ‘Coke’ and ‘Pepsi’ were also launched. The term Root Beer is derived from the reference to the brown bezel of the watch. The model was first introduced in the 1970s before Rolex rolled out the limited edition in 2018 for $21,936.

Showcasing his personality with every watch he wears, Dwyane Johnson has a mature and elegant collection that includes sturdy straps and big dials. He preserves the classics and shows off his timepieces during important events. With movies Black Adam and DC League Of Super-Pets set for 2022 releases, the actor will be sporting some of his exceptional watches for the press junket and premieres.
