'90 Day Fiance' Wife to Groom: I'm Going to Pose Naked

A model spouse? Anfisa tells Jorge that she’ll do whatever it takes to become a household name in 90 Day Fiancé‘s Sunday, September 18, episode, as seen in Us Weekly‘s exclusive sneak peek.

The TLC unscripted series‘ preview clip shows Anfisa telling her beau Jorge that she wants a career on the runway, which she hadn’t mentioned before she left her native Russia to join him in the U.S.

“I want to be a model,” she tells him. “Like bikini model. … Like a Playboy model.” She then explains to the camera, “I would like to be a model because I want to be famous. I want people to know me. I want people on the streets to know me, to want to take a picture with me.”

But her man isn’t so sure that he likes this more fame-hungry side to her. “Anfisa comes off as a little bit too focused on money,” Jorge says. “I’m hoping she’ll grow out of that phase once we get married.”

The two later butt heads when Anfisa explains why she almost didn’t leave Russia to join him in the States. “You were pissing me off,” she tells him. “Then I changed my mood because I don’t have to stay here — I can go back to Russia if I don’t like something. I thought that I can come and see how things are here, and if anything [goes wrong], I can go back.”

Watch the clip in the video above to hear Anfisa tell Jorge how much of his money she believes he should let her spend each month. 90 Day Fiancé airs on TLC Sundays at 9 p.m. ET.
