Amanda Bynes Comes For Drake Again

The digital saga between Amanda Bynes and her feelings for Drake continue. After explicitly asking for the D then flipping the script to write off his looks as “ugly,” Bynes took to timelines to hail the Young Money rapper once more.

“Drake Is The Most Gorgeous Man On The Face Of The Earth,” she wrote Wednesday night (July 3), though she admits he ain’t the only one. “I’m In Love With Someone Else Who I Do Not Follow On Twitter Who Is The Most Gorgeous Man I Know But Drake Comes In Second.”

Last week, the aspiring rap star—who may be working with Waka Flocka Flame—also showered him with e-ffection. “I Know I Love Drake Because If He Got Parkinson’s Or Got Into Some Sort Of Accident & He Looked Different He’d Still Be The Only One I Want,” she wrote, before adding, “Drake Is The Man Of My Dreams.”

According to E! News, Bynes also wrote that Drake reciprocated the feelings. “Nothing offends me more than being compared to Perez Hilton! Drake and I have been talking for years and have mutual crushes on each other!,” she posted.

She continued her flip-flopper love note. “Drake Is Not The One For Me, But I’m Sure He’ll Make Someone Happy!,” she allegedly wrote before reverting back to her original thoughts on the rapper, “Drake Is Ugly.”

While those characters appear to have evaporated from her timeline, the She’s The Man star did explain her other obsession: plastic surgery.” I Need To Have Surgery To Look Beautiful For The Man I’m In Love With,” she writes, “So I Feel Comfortable With The Way I Look When We Get Married.”

Bynes even channels Ghandi, saying, “You Have To Make A Change To See A Change.”
