Andrei Sison Death Occured In A Tragic Car Accident: Obituary Details

Sparkle GMA Artist Center, the talent agency of GMA teen artist Andrei Sison, announced on social media that the young artist had passed away in a car accident in the early morning of Friday, March 24. S

sparkle offered their sincere condolences to Andrei’s family and asked that his fans respect the family’s privacy during this difficult time. 

Andrei was a well-loved member of the Sparkle family, and the agency expressed their sadness at his passing. Andrei was only 16 years old at the time of the accident.

According to reports, Andrei had finished taping for a variety show at GMA Studios on Thursday night, March 23, and left the studio before 7 pm. 

The accident occurred at 2:30 am on Friday, and Sparkle received a call from Andrei’s relatives at around 5:30 am informing them of the tragedy. The police have yet to release details on the accident.

Andrei was a talented young artist who charmed his followers with his TikTok dance covers and live streams with other Sparkle artists. 

He was highly regarded within the entertainment industry and society as a whole. His parents instilled in him the values of hard work and dedication, which helped him achieve his goals at a young age. 

Despite their busy schedules, Andrei’s parents always made time for him and supported him in everything he did.

In the wake of his passing, the entire entertainment industry is mourning the loss of Andrei Sison. His legacy will live on through his work and the memories he has left behind. 

Fans have left numerous comments on his most recent Instagram photo from March 11, expressing their sorrow and shock at his untimely death. 

In this time of great grief, Sparkle GMA Artist Center asked that everyone respect Andrei’s family’s privacy and join them in praying for the eternal repose of his soul.
