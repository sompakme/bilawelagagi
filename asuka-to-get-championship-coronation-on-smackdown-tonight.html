Asuka To Get Championship Coronation On SmackDown Tonight

WWE.com revealed that Asuka will be getting a championship coronation on tonight’s WWE SmackDown when the blue brand goes to Fresno, CA. Asuka won the SmackDown Women’s Championship in what was an extremely memorable main event at WWE TLC as she faced Charlotte Flair and the former champion in Becky Lynch. WWE.com posted the following excerpt below:

Asuka emerged victorious in the first-ever Women’s TLC Match on Sunday night, becoming the new SmackDown Women’s Champion. Though she got an unexpected assist from Raw Women’s Champion Ronda Rousey, The Empress of Tomorrow has declared that she is now “the man” after defeating Becky Lynch and Charlotte Flair.
