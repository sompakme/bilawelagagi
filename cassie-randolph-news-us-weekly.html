Cassie Randolph News - Us Weekly

Name:Cassie Randolph

Hometown:Huntington Beach, California

Relationship:Single

Birthday:April 27, 1995

About

Cassie Randolph grew up in Huntington Beach, California, where she was an avid surfer. She received her bachelor’s degree in communication sciences and disorders from Biola University in La Mirada, California, in 2016. She went on to work as an ESL substitute teacher at Huntington Beach Adult School before pursuing a master’s degree in speech pathology at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire.

Randolph pressed pause on graduate school in late 2018 to compete on season 23 of The Bachelor, where she vied for former NFL player Colton Underwood’s heart. She was a frontrunner throughout the season and made it to the final three, but ended up breaking things off with Underwood ahead of their Fantasy Suite date over her fears about the fast pace of their relationship. They split for good in May 2020.
