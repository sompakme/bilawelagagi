Celebrity Weddings of 2023: Stars Who Got Married

Naomi Watts, Chrishell Stause and more celebrities tied the knot in 2023.

Watts confirmed that she wed fellow actor Billy Crudup in June. “Hitched! 🕊️🪢♥️,” she captioned an Instagram photo of her and Crudup posing outside of a New York City courthouse.

“They wanted to keep it low-key and have this moment to themselves, and they pulled it off beautifully,” a source exclusively told Us Weekly later that month, adding that the couple’s nuptials were “stress-free” and “spur of the moment.”

One month prior, Stause and her partner, G Flip, secretly tied the knot after one year of dating. “Love doesn’t always go as planned … Sometimes it’s immeasurably better,” Stause captioned an Instagram slideshow of her and G Flip throughout their romance. The end of the clip featured the twosome celebrating in a chapel after saying “I do.”
