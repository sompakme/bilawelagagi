From Bond Girl To 'Penny Dreadful': How Eva Green Earned Her Massive Net Worth

French actress Eva Green has made a name for herself playing sinister characters that keep her audiences guessing. Her distinctive looks and ability to turn her hand to almost any kind of role has made her one of the biggest names in Hollywood right now; from witches, to Bond girls, to schoolmistresses - she's played them all. Green first made her name acting on stage, something she excelled at, before transitioning to the screen in 2003 when she appeared in The Dreamers. Fans may know her best from her turn as Vesper Lynd in Casino Royale. Green has also had top billing in a string of big movies and TV shows, making her a household name.

After acting in movies for over twenty years, Eva has surely established herself in terms of demanding high fees for her acting roles. But how much is the notoriously secretive actress worth? Read on to find out.

8 Eva Green Started Her Career In Theatre

After spending time at university, Green eventually decided on an acting career and launched herself into it with the support of her mother, taking up an acting course while living in London. Green liked how acting allowed her to process her feelings and escape herself. She began auditioning everywhere, and eventually landed herself some stage roles in France. During the early 2000s, Eva appeared in several shows, to good reviews.

7 She Then Transitioned Into Screen Roles

Green made the move into movies around 2003, when she secured her breakthrough role in The Dreamers. The movie really tested Green - biting, violent, and explicitly sexual, it was everything she was wasn't and really pushed her to her artistic limits. The graphic sex scenes in the movie were a concern for her, and she worried that appearing in the film might have threatened her career and deeply upset her family. Fortunately, Eva escaped being type-cast in similar roles, and was able to further her career off the back of its huge success.

6 What Movies Has Eva Been In?

Green continued to find mainstream success in a number of big movies. Her biggest - and the one which made her a household name - was Casino Royale in 2006, where she played bond girl Vesper Lynd - a serious love interest of the spy. Other major films followed, including The Golden Compass and Antichrist.

In 2012 Eva also started working with Tim Burton, working on his vampire movie Dark Shadows alongside Johnny Depp. Green worked with Tim Burton again in 2016, appearing in his movie Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children,

5 Eva Has Also Worked On A Number Of Big TV Shows

Eva hasn't just limited herself to movies; she also earned big bucks working on a number of huge TV shows which have been aired across the globe. Green appeared in historical drama series Camelot in 2011. She also starred in Penny Dreadful for a number of years and won awards for her work on the show.

4 Green Has Had To Fight For Her Pay

The 300: Rise of an Empire actress has earned high wages for her work on these various film and TV shows - but it certainly hasn't been plain sailing getting the money she deserves.

In 2020, Eva began legal proceedings against a production company working on a movie called The Patriot. Green said she had a “pay or play” agreement, entitling her to a £800,000 ($1.04 million) fee even though the project was abandoned. She herself is being sued by the company at London’s High Court for allegedly derailing the £4 million ($5.22 million) sci-fi thriller.

3 Eva Green Is Also A Model

In addition to being a successful actress, Green has also worked as a model. Standing at 5ft 6in, and with stunning facial features - Green was easily able to carve a side career for herself in the fashion world. Green has worked for a number of major brands internationally, as part of big advertising campaigns.

2 So How Much Is Eva Green Worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Eva Green has an estimated net worth of around $10m. This figure puts her amongst many of the highest-earning actresses in Hollywood today. Impressive!

1 Eva Isn't Looking To Stop Acting Anytime Soon

Is Eva looking at slowing down and enjoying her riches a little more? Not at all. She's still enjoying her experience acting in big budget productions.

"I’m still getting fun from it, the sort of fun a child gets from doing something they love and that is a little bit naughty,” she said in an interview with The Guardian.

Eva maintains that she's not interested in living a wild life, something which may surprise people who have seen her movies.

“I’m quiet – boring even – in real life. Nothing like the mad witches I play," she jokes.

We'll have to wait and see which exciting roles Eva turns her hand to next!
