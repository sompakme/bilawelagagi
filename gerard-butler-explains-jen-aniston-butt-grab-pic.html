Gerard Butler recently got very touchy-feely with his The Bounty Hunter costar Jen Aniston — and there’s photographic proof.

During a Friday appearance on syndicated radio show “Johnjay and Rich,” Butler, 40, joked about the bootylicious snapshot.

Yet a Butler pal counters in the new Us Weekly that the flirty costars have been “hooking up for a while.” Another source confirms that the pair slept together during her February birthday blowout in Mexico.
