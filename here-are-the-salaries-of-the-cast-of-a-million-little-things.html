Here Are The Salaries Of The Cast Of 'A Million Little Things'

ABC shows never disappoint. As proof of it, the network's new dating show, The Celebrity Dating Game, has captivated many viewers. However, there's another series that has enjoyed great popularity. Once A Million Little Things premiered, it became a massive hit for the network. The show's season 4 just dropped, and it was a bittersweet moment for most fans as Stephanie Szostak's exit was finally confirmed after its premiere. Since last season, Szostak's departure from the series has been much speculated. The actress faced obstacles traveling between the USA and Canada during the pandemic to shoot for the show. In the meantime, her character, Delilah Dixon, faced a similar problem.

The pandemic was also an obstacle inside the reel world of A Million Little Things as she remained in France for most of season 3 to compromise for Szostak's filming issues. As the actress was also looking after her parents, her character too had her hands full with tending to her sick father, who later passed away from a stroke in the season 4 premiere. The storyline was clearly setting things up for a smooth exit for the character as Szostak returns to her home only to announce that she wanted to take all her children back to France where they could have a fresh start. Since then, fans have wanted to know more about the actors behind the characters. Here are the salaries of the cast of A Million Little Things.

How Much Does the Cast of 'A Million Little Things' Make?

The cast has had a pretty successful career. However, who is the richest? According to Celebrity Net Worth, it's a tie between Ron Livingston, who plays Jon Dixon in the series, and Stephanie Szostak, better known as Delilah Dixon in A Million Little Things. Both actors have amassed a fortune of $6 million, which is pretty impressive. Besides playing a married couple, the stars now have another thing in common.

The cast member that occupies third place regarding its earnings it's Allison Miller. The actress touched fans' hearts with her brilliant and sincere performance as Maggie Bloom. Her character's breast cancer battle has been one of the most memorable parts of the show. Women diagnosed with breast cancer suffer a lot. As proof of it, singer Kylie Minogue bared her soul about how breast cancer changed everything. Miller has a net worth of $5 million.

Another impressive cast member is Romany Malco, who plays Rome Howard on the ABC primetime one-hour drama. The actor has been nominated for several awards, including an NAACP Image Award, MTV Movie Award, and Screen Actors Guild Award. Malco portrayed Conrad Shepard on the Showtime series Weeds and has amassed a fortune of $4 million.

How about the other main characters? David Giuntoli, who plays Eddie Saville, a man who has to fight his own demons, has an estimated net worth of $3 million. Meanwhile, Chance Hurstfield, who became a fan favorite for playing Danny Dixon, has a fortune of $2 million, which is a good income for a 15-year-old actor.

Did 'A Million Little Things' Say Goodbye To Another Key Character?

People who love the show might question many things after A Million Little Things returned to their TV screens on September 22, 2021. Moreover, one of the most asked questions on the internet is: Did Danny leave the show? Fans love the tear-jerker show was it portrays some real, painful moments that everyone can relate to. The show's title itself is a nod to the famous saying, "Friendship isn't a big thing- it's a million little things." As the new season aired on the TV, viewers were impatient to know what the story holds in the future.

Fans have been worried about the absence of Danny in the show and wondering if he would return to A Million Little Things. In the series, Danny played the son of Jon and Delilah and had been staying by his widow mother's side after Jon died of suicide. Delilah was not around the whole season 3 as she had traveled to Europe to care for her father, who was ill. However, she couldn't return to the sets due to the COVID-19 pandemic and got stuck there. Hence, the writers got a bit creative and changed the storyline to run the show.

Did Danny Leave 'A Million Little Things' Or Is He Coming Back?

Season 4 premiere showed Delilah traveling to France to care for her father, and Danny left with her. Hence, fans were worried if Danny left the show forever. They were also concerned if some real-world problem kept him from playing his role in the drama series like Danny. Nonetheless, he is likely to come back as the creator, DJ Nash, stated that he could not tell if Danny's coming back as they're putting it up in the story. At least, this statement sounds promising to the fans so they can hope to see their favorite character back on the show real soon.
