How Jenna Dewan Made Her Colossal Net Worth And The Truth About Her Spending

Highlights

  • Jenna Dewan's success in the entertainment industry has led to a net worth of $30 million, with a significant portion attributed to her divorce settlement.
  • Dewan's career as a dancer, actress, producer, author, and model has contributed to her wealth and fame over the years.
  • With a large social media following, Dewan can earn up to $250,000 per Instagram post as a brand ambassador, enhancing her financial status.

Dancer and actress, Jenna Dewan has been going non-stop since 2006. While she is best known for playing 'Nora' in the first Step Up movie in 2006, Dewan has diversified and worked in many other things.

Dewan has made more headlines in the last couple of years though, especially for her divorce from ex-husband, Channing Tatum. It was reported that Dewan might have made from $20 to $25 million from her divorce settlement. With a current net worth of $30 million, it is possible that a bulk of her fortune is thanks (mainly) to that settlement. However, given the time that Dewan has been in the entertainment industry, some of those millions were self-made as well.

Here's how Jenna Dewan made her colossal net worth and the truth about her spending.

6 Jenna Dewan Was A TV Show Host And Judge

Jenna Dewan hosted World of Dance and Come Dance with Me

Dewan was the host of the TV show, World of Dance. She was also a judge in the reality competition television series, Come Dance with Me, and sometimes an occasional dancer on the show.

While judges on The Voice get a salary that ranges from $8 million to $25 million per season, it is unlikely that Dewan has made a similar amount of money for being a judge on Come Dance with Me. However, given Dewan's popularity as a host, she likely made a good paycheck from the show.

5 Jenna Dewan Is A Producer

Jenna Dewan co-founded 33andOut Productions

In 2018, Dewan was an executive producer on the TV series, Step Up: High Water. She's also a co-founder of a production company called '33andOut Productions'.

Along with her ex-husband, Channing Tatum, and their friends Adam Martingano, Reid Carolin, and Brett Rodriguez, Dwean started the production company. How this was broken up during the divorce is unknown.

4 Jenna Dewan Is A Published Author

Jenna Dewan's book, Gracefully You: Finding Beauty and Balance in the Everyday was a best-seller

Dewan is the proud author of a self-help book called Gracefully You: Finding Beauty and Balance in the Everyday. She wrote and published it after her divorce from Tatum.

In the book, she discusses her divorce, style, fashion, and even relationships.

While it's undisclosed how many copies of the book were sold, it's known that it was a bestseller. So it's reasonable to think that she has made a good deal of money from this book.

3 Jenna Dewan Is A Model

Jenna Dewan has been on many magazine covers for her beauty and her fitness

It's wild to think that such an amazing actress and dancer wouldn't be a model. As a young woman, Dewan was modeling. Now, she's the face of many campaigns and on magazine covers.

Some of the magazine covers that Dewan has been on include:

  • Cosmopolitan
  • The Knot
  • Health
  • Redbook
  • Women's Health

Models on the cover of huge magazines like Vogue can make from $18,000 to $300,000 a year. To guess that Dewan has made between $18,000 to $100,000 for her magazine covers would make sense.

2 Jenna Dewan Is A Social Media Celebrity And Brand Ambassador

Jenna Dewan can make up to $250,000 per Instagram post

With more than 9.5 million Instagram followers, it's no shock that Dewan is probably making a ton of money on Instagram just by posting. As a matter of fact, all celebrities get paid just to post. This is especially true if they're promoting a brand, which Dewan has done many times on her Instagram account.

As a result of her of social media work, Dewan can make from $100,000 to $250,000 per post, as many influencers do.

Brand ambassadors who have more than 7 million followers can make more than $150,000 per post just by posting on Instagram. This might as well be one of the main sources of income for Dewan.

Some of the brands that Dewan has collaborated with over the years include:

  • Danskin
  • Iconix
  • Young Living Essential Oils
  • NEOSTRATA
  • All Modern

While is is estimated that Dewan makes six-figures for being an ambassador, it has not been disclosed what Dewan has made for her work with the various companies she has collaborated with.

1 This Is How Jenna Dewan Spends Her Money

Jenna Dewan purchased a mansion and several high-end cars with her fortune

In 2021, Dewan bought (with her fiancée, Steve Kazee) a $4.7 million mansion in Encino, LA. Dewan has taken Hello Magazine inside her Encino Mansion, and her house is truly worth the money.

It is known about Dewan that she loves cars. She likes all sorts of vehicles and loves to collect them. Some of the cars that Dewan has purchased over the years include:

  • Porsche 911
  • Bentley Continental GT
  • Jaguar F-Type

It is obvious that Dewan loves to pamper herself and buy luxurious items for herself and her family.
