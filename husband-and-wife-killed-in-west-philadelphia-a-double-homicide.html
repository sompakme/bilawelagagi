Husband And Wife Killed In West Philadelphia: A Double Homicide

In a devastating incident that shook the Cobbs Creek section of West Philadelphia on July 23, 2021, a husband and wife were killed in west Philadelphia.

Their loved ones, friends, and the community as a whole are in a state of shock as a result of the distressing incident that occurred at approximately 2:45 a.m.

After getting a disturbing call, specialists immediately showed up at the scene, just to find the dead assemblages of the couple lying on the walkway close to their home.

The fact that at least 15 attackers fired multiple shots demonstrates the attack’s deliberate nature, indicating that it was not an accident.
