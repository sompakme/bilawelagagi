Johnny Buss Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Johnny Hatten Buss (born October 18, 1956) is a part-owner and the Los Angeles Lakers’ vice president of strategic development in the National Basketball Association (NBA). He served as president of the Los Angeles Lazers professional indoor soccer club and the Los Angeles Sparks professional women’s basketball team in the past. Jerry Buss, the former owner of the Los Angeles Lakers, is his father. Jerry’s 66 percent controlling ownership of the Lakers was transferred to his six children through a trust after his death in 2013, with each kid having an equal vote.
