Julian Williams net worth: Julian Williams is an American professional boxer who has a net worth of $1 million. Julian Williams was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in April 1990. He is a light middleweight who made his professional boxing debut with a win over Antonio Chaves in May 2010. Williams competed in a no contest against Hugo Centeno Jr. in September 2013 for the vacant WBC International light middleweight title. He suffered his first loss against Jermall Charlo in December 2016 for the IBF light middleweight title. In May 2019 Julian Williams defeated Jarrett Hurd to win the WBA (Super), IBF, and IBO light middleweight titles and bring his professional boxing record to 27-1-1 with 16 knockouts.  He has defeated the likes of Ishe Smith, Luciano Cuello, Joey Hernandez, Michael Medina, Freddy Hernandez, Joachim Alcine, Dashon Johnson, and many others during his career.
