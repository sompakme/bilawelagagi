Kim Kardashian Claps Back At Bette Midler, Chloe Grace Moretz On Twitter

In a very un-Kim Kardashian like move, the reality star has taken a cue out of her husband Kanye West’s book, and clapped back at Bette Midler, Piers Morgan and 19-year-old Chloe Grace Moretz for throwing unsubtle shade at her for posting a nude selfie.

To catch you up, Midler, Moretz (who has acted in films like Kick-Ass and Carrie) and Morgan have all tweeted their disapproval of Kim’s censored nude selfie, which was posted to the site on Sunday (Mar. 6).

she’s gonna have to swallow the camera.

— Bette Midler (@BetteMidler) March 7, 2016

— Chloë Grace Moretz (@ChloeGMoretz) March 7, 2016

@KimKardashian – just our bodies.

— Chloë Grace Moretz (@ChloeGMoretz) March 7, 2016

I know the old man’s $50 million in debt, Kim – but this is absurd.
Want me to buy you some clothes? https://t.co/hEA9osB2QF

— Piers Morgan (@piersmorgan) March 7, 2016

Well, late last night, Kardashian was out for their blood. Before ripping into the disapproving celebrities, she started off by noting she was “late to the party” because she was busy covering her husband’s debt and cashing an $80 million check.

Then, the fun began. She ranted that Morgan’s offer to buy clothes for her was “on some Ashley Madison type sh*t,” added that Midler (who she implied may have been sleeping due to her age) should send her nudes, and that Moretz had a “cute” Nylon Magazine cover shot, but no one knows who she is. She concluded with posting yet another nude shot with the hashtag “#liberated.”

Ouch.

Kim, who usually seems a bit more level-headed, may have had a bit of help with her tweets from a certain someone, but regardless, this was unexpected. Midler and Moretz have not responded, but Piers Morgan has never been one to hold his tongue. He tweeted that he is “fairly sure” that Kanye tweeted for Kim, and also wrote a piece for the Daily Mail about it.
