Kylie Jenner Focuses On Her Kids And Perfume Launch As Timothee Chalamet Fuels Break-Up Rumors

Highlights

  • Kylie's social media silence hints at trouble with Timothee Chalamet, leading fans to speculate on their relationship status.
  • Timothee's absence from Kylie's TikTok video fuels break-up theories, as he hasn't been featured before despite public appearances.
  • Reports suggest Kylie's jealousy and strict rules may have strained her relationship with Timothee, causing speculation about their future.

Kylie Jenner continues to stay quiet amid reports she broke up with Timothee Chalamet, but her silence is speaking volumes. The makeup mogul’s recent social media behavior continues to leave fans convinced that something is amiss between the couple. Plus, Timothee’s recent appearances to promote Dune: Part 2 are also fuelling speculation.

Break-up theories intensified following Kylie’s recent upload on her TikTok account featuring her daughter Stormi, 6, and son Aire, 2, along with her sister Kendall Jenner, 28. Notably absent from the video, however, was Timothée, leading to various questions in the comments about what their status is.

Fans Wonder Why Kylie Is Leaving Timothee Off Social Media

In the TikTok, Kylie shared what a normal day in her life looks like while spending the day at home with her kids. Dressed in a sleek black outfit, the reel begin with Kylie preparing pancakes for her children, whom she shares with her ex Travis Scott.

Following breakfast, Kylie showed her followers her strict skincare regimen before spending quality time with her son Aire on a trampoline. Later, Kylie hits the gym before joining her sister Kendall for an excursion to the stables, where they tend to their horses and enjoy a ride around the premises.

Timothee’s absence in the clip isn’t necessarily unusual, given that Kylie hasn’t featured him on her socials before, despite many PDA-heavy public appearances, confirming their relationship status.

Timothee has been engaging in various promotional appearances recently to promote the new Dune sequel, including a red carpet moment where he wore matching outfits with Zendaya. But Kylie’s absence from such PR events has furthered break-up speculation. Likewise, Timothee hasn't said anything about Kylie's latest business venture - her perfume launch - which has been occupying a lot of her time, or at least her attention on social media, given her recent posts.

Moreover, it's been nearly three months since Kylie and Timothee were last publicly spotted together. Their last appearance was at the Golden Globes in Beverly Hills on January 7, where they allegedly incurred drama with Selena Gomez after Kylie seemed to not allow her boyfriend to take a photo with the actress.

Kylie’s Rules May Have Driven Timothee Away

There have been various reports suggesting that Timothee was struggling to adapt to Kylie’s lifestyles and expectations. One report claimed the Kylie Cosmetics founder was jealous of Timothee’s past romantic interests and even demanded to know his social media passwords, possibly putting strain on their new relationship.

“First, Kylie wanted him to cut ties with all his exes, even the ones he still considers friends,” a source claimed to Heat Magazine. “He’s dated a lot more people than anyone realises, including the likes of Lily Rose Depp and [Madonna’s daughter] Lourdes Leon. But Kylie’s suspicious of any women in his life, even those he works with.”

The source said Kylie has a “long – and, at times, bizarre – list of dos and don’ts.” They said, “Allegedly, Timothée has also given Kylie his social media passwords and shares his location with her 24/7. He’s obviously under her spell and jumps to her defence when his friends give him a hard time.”

Though Kylie and Timothee are keeping tight-lipped about what’s going on between them, the longer they stay silent, the more it appears to give fans reason to believe something isn’t right between them.
