LeBron James, Channing Tatum Host Fundraiser On 'Ellen' For I Promise School

LeBron James and Channing Tatum stopped by Ellen to raise money for the former’s new school, I Promise Elementary in his hometown of Akron, Ohio.

The pair completed five hilarious dares to increase funds in partnership with Walmart. To begin the festivities, the former Cleveland Cavalier took a shot of tequila without using his hands, while Tatum had to perform his sexiest crawl for the audience.

With each dare worth $10,000, the duo completed a handful of gestures where James was blindfolded and forced to lick an unknown object, that ended up being a kiwi.

As the segment came to an end, the entertainers had two final dares to complete. James was asked to eat spoonfuls of ice cream drenched in Tabasco hot sauce. “Listen kids back home, LeBron loves you,” he playfully said moments before he took two big bites of the concoction.

As the duo accumulated over five completed dares, they raised more than $100,000 for the learning facility that will go toward its students’ education.
