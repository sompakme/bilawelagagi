Natalie Anderson Career

Anderson’s acting career started in 1996 when she made her television debut as Francesca in The Biz.  In 2001, she made the Top 50 in the original series of Pop Idol but narrowly missed out on a place in the live finals after finishing third in her group.

During 2005 and 2008, she played a lead role and having several storylines based around her character, Nurse Stella Davenport in the ITV drama series The Royal. On November 24, 2008, she released her debut album ‘Return to Me’.

In 2009, Anderson appeared as Ewa in the British film Freight which was released in December 2009.

Her most recent stage role was as Nessarose in the London production of Wicked. She replaced Caroline Keiff on 11 May 2009 and left at the end of her contract on 27 March 2010. She was replaced by Cassandra Compton.

In 2010, Anderson joined the cast of Emmerdale, and she played Alicia Gallagher, Leyla Harding’s (Roxy Shahidi) sister. However, two years later, in May 2012, she saw it right to take a break from the film to go on maternity leave—Alicia’s exit storyline involved her getting arrested for assault. Alicia returned later that year.

In 2013, Anderson took part in the ITV entertainment show, Your Face Sounds Familiar. She sang as Katy Perry, Britney Spears, Kate Bush, Justin Bieber, Jessie J, and Mariah Carey while she was on the show. She won the series on 3 August 2013.

In 2014, Anderson appeared in an episode of Fake Reaction on ITV2 and Keith Lemon’s Through the Keyhole, and in February that year appeared on the ITV entertainment show, All-Star Family Fortunes with her family, who were competing against Steve Redgrave and his family.

On 27 September 2014, Anderson appeared in a celebrity episode of The Chase.
