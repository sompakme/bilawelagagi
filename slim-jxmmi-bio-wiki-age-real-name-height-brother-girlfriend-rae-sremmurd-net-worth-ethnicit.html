Slim Jxmmi Biography – Slim Jxmmi Wiki

Slim Jxmmi real name Aaquil Iben Shamon Brown, is an American Recording artist who is part of the Hip hop duo, Rae Sremmurd.

Slim Jxmmi Age

He was born on December 29, 1993 in Inglewood, California, United States.

Slim Jxmmi Family

Slim Jxmmi and his younger brother Swae Lee born Khalif Malik Ibn Shaman Brown, grew up with their mother, Bernadette Walker, and their brother, Michael, their father having abandoned his family a few years after the birth of his sons. He has a stepbrother named Floyd Jr. Sullivan.
