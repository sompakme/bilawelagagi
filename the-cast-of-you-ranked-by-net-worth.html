The Cast Of 'You' Ranked By Net Worth

The show You has become one of Netflix's most successful shows in recent years. Penn Badgley stars as Joe Goldberg, a serial killer and bookstore manager who is obsessed with a woman he will do anything to get close to. During the show's run, it has been nominated for MTV Movie & TV Awards, Saturn Awards, Artios Awards, Hollywood Critics Association TV Awards, and MTV Movie & TV Awards.

The show has run for three seasons, with the fourth season set to premiere in February 2023. The cast has received acclaim for their performances in the show, especially Badgley. But which cast member has the highest net worth?

Let's find out.

8 Ambyr Childers - $500 Thousand

Childers plays the role of Candace Stone in the show. She is no stranger to television. Childers played the role of Colby Chandler in the popular soap opera All My Children from 2006 to 2008. She also starred in the television series Ray Donovan from 2013 to 2016, where she played the role of Ashley Rucker. During her time on Ray Donovan, Childers starred as Sadie on the series Aquarius. She has also made several film appearances including Carolina, Dickie Roberts: Former Child Star, Love, Lay the Favorite, Playback, The Master, Gangster Squad, Broken City, We Are What We Are, 2 Guns, Vice, and Pinocchio.

7 Tati Gabrielle - $1.5 Million

Gabrielle plays the role of Marienne Bellamy on the show. She also appeared in the show Chilling Adventures of Sabrina as Prudence Night. She began her career in 2016 when she appeared in the movie Just Jenna, playing the role of Monique. She also appeared in television series such as K.C. Undercover, The Thundermans, and Dimension 404. Gabrielle was also included in The 100 in 2017, a sci-fi drama series. She played the role of Gaia. Gabrielle has also worked as a voice actor in projects such as Tarantula and The Owl House.

6 Elizabeth Lail - $2 Million

Lail plays the role of Beck in the show. Lail broke through with her role as Princess Anna in the fantasy drama series Once Upon a Time. She also played the role of Amy Hughes in the supernatural horror series Dead of Summer. Lail attended the University of North Carolina School of the Arts, where she received a Bachelor of Fine Arts in 2014. One of Lail's earliest appearances was in 2011, when she appeared in the short film Model Airplane, playing the role of Anna. She also played the role of Quinn Harris in the 2019 film Countdown. Lail also appears in the revival of the popular series Gossip Girl.

5 Jenna Ortega - $3 Million

Ortega plays the role of Ellie Alves on the show. In addition to You, she has appeared in other television series, including Elena of Avalor, where she played Princess Isabel. Ortega also appeared as the voice of Princess Isabel in the miniseries Elena of Avalor: Adventures in Vallestrella in 2017, reprising her role. Ortega has also appeared in several films including Iron Man 3, Insidious: Chapter 2, and The Little Rascals Save the Day. She also appeared in the show Rake in 2014, where she played the role of Zoe Leon. Ortega won the Imagen Award for Best Young Actor – Television in 2018 for her role in Stuck in the Middle. She currently stars in the Netflix smash hit Wednesday.

4 Victoria Pedretti - $5 Million

Pedretti plays the role of Love Quinn in the show. She achieved her breakthrough thanks to her role in the Netflix series The Haunting of Hill House, where she played Eleanor “Nell” Crain. Pedretti received nominations at both the Saturn Awards and MTV Awards for her role. She also played Lulu in the Quentin Tarantino film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. Her acting career began in 2014 when she appeared in two independent short films. In 2017, Pedretti received a Bachelor of Fine Arts in acting from the Carnegie Mellon School of Drama in Pittsburgh.

3 Shay Mitchell - $6 Million

Mitchell plays the role of Peach Salinger on the show. Her career began in her late teens when she began modeling. Before that, Mitchell was a competitive dancer during her childhood. She eventually moved into acting, guest-starring on Degrassi: The Next Generation in 2009. She proceeded to take guest-starring roles in the shows Aaron Stone and Rookie Blue. Her most famous role to date came when she appeared as Emily Fields on the series Pretty Little Liars. Mitchell has also been featured on shows like Punk'd and Glee. Mitchell is also a spokesperson for the Pantene Nature Fusion line.

2 Penn Badgley - $8 Million

Badgley plays the role of Joe Goldberg on the show. While he has achieved great success for this role, he is also known for being part of the hit series Gossip Girl, playing Dan Humphrey. Badgley has acted in the films John Tucker Must Die, The Stepfather, Easy A, and Margin Call. He's also appeared in television series such as The Young and the Restless, Do Over, The Mountain, and The Bedford Diaries. He became a producer on You in its third season and executive produced Moth, a short film released in 2009.

1 Saffron Burrows - $10 Million

Burrows plays the role of Dottie Quinn on the show. She began her career as a model, before moving into film and television in the early 90s. Her various acting credits include In the Name of the Father, Circle of Friends, Karaoke, Cold Lazarus, Crucial Takes, Hotel de Love, Nevada, One Night Stand, The Matchmaker, Wing Commander, Deep Blue Sea, Miss Julie, Timecode, Gangster No 1, Enigma, Tempted, Hotel, The Seventh Stream, Frida, Flashpoint, Troy, and more. Burrows has also appeared in the hit television series Angels of S.H.I.E.L.D.
