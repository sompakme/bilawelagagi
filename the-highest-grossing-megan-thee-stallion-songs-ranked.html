The Highest-Grossing Megan Thee Stallion Songs, Ranked

Grammy Winning Rapper and singer Megan Thee Stallion rose to prominence after her freestyling videos went viral on social media platforms. She signed with 300 Entertainment in 2018, and there has been no looking back since then. Megan released her first mixtape titled Fever in 2019, followed by extended play Suga in 2020, featured in the Top 10 of the Billboard 200. She collaborated with A-listers like Beyoncé, Doja Cat, Cardi B, etc., and has already established herself as a successful rapper and singer.

Megan Thee Stallion has cemented her position in the music industry with several chart-busting songs, including Thot Shit, Big Ole Freak, Hot Girl Summer, Savage, etc.

Cry Baby - Peak Position #28

Cry Baby was released on November 20, 2020, as the third track from Megan Thee Stallion's debut studio album, Good News. It featured Megan alongside fellow rapping sensation DaBaby. Cry Baby is a bass-heavy track featuring rappers discussing their busy sex lives. The song was featured on the Billboard Hot 100 charts for 20 Weeks reaching a peak position of 28. The song was well received by the critics leading to a music video in 2021 that featured Megan and DaBaby as dolls.

Thot Shit - Peak Position #16

The lead single from Megan Thee Stallion’s 2021 album Something for Thee Hotties, Thot Sit, was nominated for Best Rap Performance at the 64th Annual Grammy Awards. Megan performed the song as her aggressive alter-ego Tina Saw, and it went on to get featured in the Billboard Hot 100, trending at No.16 the very day it was released. It continued to stay on the chart for 20 Weeks.

Sweetest Pie - Peak Position #15

Sweetest Pie is the lead single of Megan Thee Stallion’s second studio album Traumazine. The song also featured Dua Lipa and was released on March 11, 2022. The song debuted at No.15 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, making it Dua Lipa’s highest Billboard debut as per UPROXX. The music video was released at the same time as the song and was inspired by Hansel And Gretel.

Beautiful Mistakes - Peak Position #13

Beautiful Mistakes is a 2021 song by the extremely popular Maroon 5, featuring young rap icon Megan Thee Stallion. The pop-rock song is composed with heavy drums and synth and was released as the third single of Maroon 5’s 7th studio album Jordi. The song received primarily negative reviews, with Variety listing it as one of the worst songs of 2021. Yet the song marched into the Billboard Hot 100 chart and peaked at No.13.

Body - Peak Position #12

Body debuted at No.12 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and is one of the most popular Megan Thee Stallion songs. It is the third single from her debut album Good News released in 2020. Megan penned down the song and based it on her body during the pandemic. The song was accompanied by a music video featuring cameos by Taraji P. Henson, Blac Chyna, etc. Body featured in the Billboard Hot 100 chart for around 20 weeks, as per Billboard.

34+35 (Remix) - Peak Position #2

34+35 is a chartbuster single by Ariana Grande, released in 2020. The song became an instant chartbuster with subtle references to sex and double entendres. The song's widespread popularity led to a remix featuring Megan Thee Stallion and Doja Cat in 2021. 34+35, which initially debuted at No.8 on the Billboard Hot 100, went up to a whopping No.2 in January 2021 after the remix's release and continued to feature on the chart for 28 weeks.

WAP - Peak Position #1

Released in 2020 by Atlantic Records, WAP is a chartbuster song by Cardi B with guest vocals by Megan Thee Stallion. The song debuted at No.1 on the Billboard Hot 100 and received rave reviews from critics and the public. It did receive some flak due to its sexually explicit lyrics but achieved the largest opening streaming week in the US, as per Vulture. WAP even had a star-studded music video, including cameos by Kylie Jenner, Normani, and Rosalia. It topped the Billboard charts for four weeks and remained on the list for 24 weeks.

Savage - Peak Position #1

Probably Megan Thee Stallion's most successful song to date, Savage, was released in 2020 as a part of her extended play, Suga. The piece opened to widespread critical acclaim, and later a remix was released featuring music sensation Beyoncé. Savage topped the Billboard Hot 100 charts on May 30, 2020, making it Megan’s first No.1 single and Beyonce’s 7th. The song went on to bag Two Grammy awards for Best Rap Performance and Best Rap Song.

Through her extensive talent, Megan Thee Stallion has become one of the leading female rappers in the industry. At the age of just 28, she already has several chart-busting songs to her name, which also includes two chart toppers. Savage and WAP are the most successful songs of Megan’s career, peaking at #1 in the Billboard Hot 100 for one week and four weeks, respectively.
