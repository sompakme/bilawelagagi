The Lost Sons: Who Are Paul Fronczak Parents? Meet His Mother Dora Fronczak And Father Chester

Paul Fronczak finally succeeded in knowing that he was not the real baby Paul that his parents lost 56 years ago.

Paul Fronczak is a man in his mid-fifties who works in a non-profit organization.

The story related to Paul is a rather interesting and a bit confusing one as you may say.

But it provides the feeling of a movie story with unexpected twists and turns that you begin to doubt if it is even real.

The investigation story details a newborn baby who gets stolen just a day after his birth which was recently shown in the film The Lost Sons.

Baby Paul, the main character of the story gets stolen from Michael Reese in 1964 and more than a half-century later, the actual truth unfolds.

Here we take a look at who are the actual parents of Paul Fronczak and such other related details.

Paul Fronczak Parents: Meet His Mother Dora Fronczak And Father Chester Fronczak

The actual Paul Fronczak was born to his mother Dora Fronczak and father Chester Fronczak.

Paul was born to his parents in Michael Reese hospital in 1964 but was stolen the very next day by a woman who dressed as a nurse.

The incident became national news and later a baby thought to be as Paul Fronczak was found abandoned in New Zersey.

Unsure if the found one was their actual baby, Dora and Chester adopted the baby and raised it as Paul but it turns out the found baby was a random one.

The now known Paul Fronczak grew up among his parents and a sibling but he didn’t look like any of them and felt alone from his teenage years.

Eventually, in 2012, the DNA testing proved that he was not the actual Paul and the hunt for the lost baby began.

Where Is Paul Fronczak Now?

The actual Paul Fronczak who was stolen from the hospital died in 2020 due to COVID.

There are two Paul in this story, the real one stolen from the hospital and the one raised as Paul by the couple Dora and Chester.

The raised Paul Fronczak is still alive and is actively involved in non for profit organizational works.

Whereas, the real Paul who was stolen, later raised as Kevin Baty, is now dead after having COVID complications.

Chicago Suntimes reports the incident which reveals that the actual Paul never got to meet his mom Dora but they talked on the phone like two times.

Dora and Paul had later arranged to meet personally but the lost baby of 1964 passed away before that.
