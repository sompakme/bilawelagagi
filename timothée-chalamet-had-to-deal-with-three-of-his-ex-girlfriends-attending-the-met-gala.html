Timothe Chalamet Had To Deal With Three Of His Ex-Girlfriends Attending The Met Gala

Timothée Chalamet co-chaired the anticipated 2021 Met Gala alongside co-chairs Billie Eilish, Amanda Gorman and Naomi Osaka on Monday. A video featuring Chalamet’s awkward time at the Met Gala went viral on TiKTok, featuring red-carpet photos of his past loves.

A blind item posted to DeuxMoi read: “Lol Timothée Chalamet definitely had an awkward time at the Met, Eiza Gonzalez, his highschool girlfriend Lourdes and Lily Rose Depp were there too.”

For Lourdes Leon - Timothée was her first ever boyfriend in highschool and they were a “little item”. While attending the Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts in Manhattan, Lourdes met Chalamet, who also happened to be a student.

Although the couple haven’t discussed their relationship much publicly, Chalamet and Leon have expressed having a mutual respect for each other. They dated for a few months in 2013 and also attended prom as a couple!

Lily-Rose Depp, who Chalamet worked with on the Netflix film The King was also present at the event. The couple began dating after meeting on the film set, and dated on-and-off between 2018 and 2020 before splitting up later that year.
