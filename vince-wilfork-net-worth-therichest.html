Currently playing for the National Football League, Vince Wilfork is a professional football player with an estimated net worth of $25 million in 2012. He is the current defensive lineman of the New England Patriots.

After playing college football at the University of Miami, Vince Wilfork was selected by the New England Patriots as its 21st overall pick in the first round of the 1994 NFL Draft. Since he joined the NFL, Wilfork has never played for any other teams aside from the Patriots.

Vince Wilfork was selected to Pro-Bowl for five times (2007, 2009, 2010, 2011 and 2012). In 2012, he became a part of the first team All-Pro. He was also part of the second All-Pro in 2007, 2009, 2010 and 2011.

In 2011, he ranked 31st in the list of Top 100 Players in 2011. A year later, he ranked 81st in the list of Top 100 Players in 2012.

Together with his wife, Bianca, daughter and two sons, Vince Wilfork currently resides in Franklin Massachusetts.Wilfork was born on November 4, 1981 in Boynton Beach, Florida.
