Virginie Ledoyen Age: 10 Facts You Should Know About

Virginie Fernandez is best known for her stage name i.e. Virginie Ledoyen. She was born on November 15, 1976, in Paris, France.

Despite being a French actress she has also worked in various British and American movies. Ledoyen is the surname she took as a souvenir from her maternal grandmother. Life was very tough for her to start into the acting field but being a print model from the age two somewhat helped her get into the sphere.

She worked on various films like Exploits of a Young Don Juan, The Children Thief, Cold Water but her career took a massive turn after she worked on A Single Girl. She got listed on the nomination list for a César Award for Most Promising Actress.

Apart from French movies, she has done commendable work on the movie named, The Beach(2000) for which she was known for the best role. She also worked on various TV shows but is remembered for her role as Cosette in the French mini television series Les Miserables.
