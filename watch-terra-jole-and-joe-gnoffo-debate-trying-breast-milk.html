Watch Terra Jole and Joe Gnoffo Debate Trying Breast Milk

Just a little taste? In Us Weekly’s exclusive sneak peek at the May 6 episode of Terra’s Little Family, Joe Gnoffo debates trying his partner Terra Jole’s breastmilk.

“From the get-go when I was pregnant he said he was going to take it straight from the tap,” Jole tells a group of friends. “When I ended up feeding and pumping he was like ‘nope I changed my mind!’”

“It looks delicious,” Gnoffo answers.

Jole challenges Gnoffo to try it with her, and brings out shot glasses of breastmilk for them to take in front of their friends.
