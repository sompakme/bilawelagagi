Who is Charli D’ Amelio?

Charli D’Amelio is an American social media personality, dancer, and TikTok star. She was born on May 1, 2004, in Norwalk, Connecticut. Charli gained popularity on TikTok in 2019 by posting dance videos to popular songs. Her relatable content, impressive dance skills, and bubbly personality earned her a massive following. Charli is currently one of the most-followed personalities on TikTok, with over 120 million followers in September 2021. She has also amassed a significant following on social media platforms such as Instagram, YouTube, and Twitter. Charli has used her social media influence to collaborate with brands, launch a podcast, and even release a book. She has become an inspiration to many young people around the world, showing that with hard work and dedication, anything is possible.
