Yes, Zachary Cruz is the adopted child of Lynda and Roger Cruz.

Zachary Cruz was adopted at birth by the couple, who raised him in a loving environment.

As of, both his adoptive parents have passed away. Roger died on August 11, 2004, at the age of 67; whereas, Lynda was 68 at the time of her demise on November 1, 2017.

Following his mother’s death, Zachary was forced to move and leave behind his school and his friends.

Who Is Zachary Cruz? Nikolas Cruz Brother

Zachary Cruz is the younger brother of Parkland school shooter Nikolas Cruz.

The younger Cruz was sentenced to six months of probation for trespassing at Parkland school in 2018. Apparently, he was being punished because of his older brother, who killed 17 people at the massacre at Marjory Stoneman Douglas High in Parkland, Florida.

Although Zachary was arrested on March 19, 2018, he was later released from the Broward County Jail on a $500,000 bond. Reportedly, his attorney argued that the excessive bond showed Cruz was being punished for his brother’s actions. He also added that he has been punished for who he is related to, not because of anything that he did.

In addition, Zachary and Nikolas also have an older sister who is currently serving an eight-year prison sentence on charges including the second-degree attempted murder of a police officer.
